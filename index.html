I am a first-year PhD student at TU Delft, where I am supervised by Savvas Zannettou and Yury Zhauniarovich. My research focuses on the safe and responsible integration of AI in digital environments, positioned at the intersection of Natural Language Processing, Information Retrieval, and Computational Social Science.

My journey is driven by a joy for exploring diverse fields and a passion for building new things. It's a path that has given me the privilege of leading research and industry teams, launching successful products, and teaching/managing large university courses. These varied experiences are the foundation of my interdisciplinary work and my aspiration to build a "Distributed Scientific Persona" —firmly anchored in computer science but deeply enriched by the social sciences and humanities. I’m always excited to connect with others who share a passion for learning and collaboration, so please don’t hesitate to get in touch if you see anything interesting here :D
